Understanding AI Concepts

Artificial Intelligence is transforming the way we live and work. In this post, I want to explore some of the fundamental concepts that drive this technology.

Machine Learning vs. Deep Learning

At its core, Machine Learning (ML) is a subset of AI that enables systems to learn from data. Deep Learning is a specialized subset of ML that uses neural networks with many layers.

Generative AI

Generative AI is a type of artificial intelligence technology that can produce various types of content, including text, imagery, audio and synthetic data.
